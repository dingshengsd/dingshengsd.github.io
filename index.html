Introduction

Dingseng group was established in 2016 in Weihai city, which covers an area of ​​12000 square meters and currently has 105 employees, including 12 engineers and 30 professional technical staffs.

Our company focuses on the field of automation, CNC machining, injection mold manufacturing and assembling products, providing one-stop solution in industrial field.

Ding Sheng is a group enterprise, which consists of two independent companies.

One is Weihai Ding Sheng SanHe Precision Machinery Co., Ltd, which is proficient in designing electronic automation production line from designing manufacturing to manufacturing. The company is capable to provide the solution in terms of clients' requirement.

The other one is Weihai Ding Sheng Intelligent Technology Co., Ltd, which specifies in the field of industrial products , outdoor exercise products, fishing gear and injection mold products. Apart from the above services, Intelligent Tech is able to assemble, sale, import and export., etc.

Quality Assurance

Quality Assurance

Quality is the strong guarantee for the development of the company, obviously, production processing is the key to product quality, inspection is the guarantee for the products. WHDS has been strictly followed SOP for production process. Our stable and qualified products have earned our customers' trust for years.

To make sure the products conform to our customers' requirements, from raw material to shipment, every step of the manufacturing process is under control. All products will be under 3 checks in the whole process:

  • Raw material inspection: check the raw material before accepting and storing.
  • In-line inspection: technicians do self-check for every parts and QC spot check during production.
  • Final inspection: QC 100% inspect the finished product before shipping and choose the best packaging way to avoid damages during transportation.
